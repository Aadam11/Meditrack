The Crisis We're Solving

4,000+

Cholera deaths in Nigeria in 2023 alone

Late outbreak detection costs lives. Traditional surveillance systems take weeks to identify disease patterns, allowing preventable epidemics to spread across communities.

MediTrack changes this by enabling real-time community reporting and AI-powered early detection to contain outbreaks within hours, not weeks.

How MediTrack Protects Nigeria

📱

Community Reporting

Simple mobile interface for citizens to report disease symptoms via SMS, WhatsApp, or app. Works on any phone, including basic feature phones.

🤖

AI Early Detection

Machine learning algorithms analyze patterns across reports to predict outbreaks 2-3 weeks before traditional methods, enabling proactive response.

🚨

Instant Alerts

Automated notifications to health authorities, WHO Nigeria, and NCDC within minutes of pattern detection, ensuring rapid deployment of response teams.

🗺️

Real-time Mapping

Live outbreak visualization across all Nigerian states with hotspot identification, resource allocation tracking, and containment zone management.

📊

Predictive Analytics

Historical data analysis to predict seasonal disease patterns, enabling preventive vaccination campaigns and resource pre-positioning.

🏥

Response Coordination

Integrated platform for health workers, government agencies, and international partners to coordinate response efforts and track intervention success.
