Our Comprehensive Solution

MediTrack is the first integrated platform that connects every stakeholder in Nigeria's health ecosystem for rapid outbreak detection and response.

Multi-Channel Reporting

Report outbreaks via mobile app, SMS, or USSD codes. Works for everyone from urban professionals to rural communities with basic phones.

AI-Powered Detection

Machine learning algorithms classify diseases, predict outbreak patterns, and automatically match resources with needs in real-time.

Smart Mapping

Interactive disease heatmaps show outbreak hotspots, resource availability, and facility status across all 774 LGAs in Nigeria.

Stakeholder Integration

Connects communities, health workers, government agencies, and NGOs on one platform for coordinated emergency response.

Verified & Secure

Healthcare worker verification, anonymous reporting options, and enterprise-grade security protect sensitive health data.

Real-Time Analytics

Live dashboards track outbreak trends, response times, resource allocation, and population health metrics for data-driven decisions.
