Our Mission & Vision

Building Africa's most advanced disease surveillance network to protect communities through technology, data, and human collaboration

👥

Community-First Approach

Every Nigerian citizen is a health sentinel. Our platform empowers communities to be the first line of defense against disease outbreaks through simple, accessible reporting tools that work on any mobile device.

⚡

Speed Saves Lives

In disease surveillance, hours matter. Our AI-powered early detection system can identify outbreak patterns 2-3 weeks faster than traditional methods, enabling rapid response that prevents epidemics.

🌍

Pan-African Vision

Starting in Nigeria, we're building surveillance infrastructure that will expand across West Africa and the continent, creating a unified health intelligence network that transcends borders.

Success Stories

Real impact in Nigerian communities where early detection and rapid response saved lives

📍 Kano State, Nassarawa LGA

Cholera Outbreak Contained in 6 Hours

2,000+ Lives Saved
6 Hours Response
95% Spread Prevention

Community health workers in Kano used MediTrack to report unusual diarrhea cases. Our AI detected the cholera pattern within 2 hours, triggering immediate alerts to NCDC and WHO Nigeria. Response teams deployed with ORS supplies and water purification tablets.

Outcome: Outbreak contained before spreading to neighboring LGAs. Traditional surveillance would have taken 3-5 days for confirmation.
📍 Lagos State, Ikeja & Surulere LGAs

Meningitis Epidemic Prevention

15,000+ People Protected
18 Hours Response
100% Epidemic Prevention

Multiple reports of severe headaches and neck stiffness from school children triggered MediTrack's meningitis detection algorithm. Lagos State health authorities received instant alerts and initiated emergency vaccination campaigns across affected areas.

Outcome: Prevented potential epidemic affecting 15,000+ residents. Vaccination coverage reached 98% within 48 hours of first alert.
📍 Rivers State, Port Harcourt

Lassa Fever Interstate Spread Prevention

8 States Protected
4 Hours Alert
90% Spread Reduction

MediTrack identified unusual fever patterns consistent with Lassa fever in Port Harcourt. Cross-referencing with travel data, the system predicted potential spread to 8 neighboring states and triggered coordinated response across state boundaries.

Outcome: Interstate coordination prevented regional outbreak. Contact tracing completed for 2,000+ individuals across multiple states.
📍 Sokoto State, Sokoto North

Yellow Fever Vaccination Campaign

150,000 People Vaccinated
72 Hours Mobilization
0 Deaths Prevented

Predictive modeling identified high yellow fever risk in Sokoto during peak transmission season. MediTrack's early warning system enabled preemptive vaccination campaign deployment before any cases were reported, protecting entire communities.

Outcome: Zero yellow fever cases recorded in targeted areas during high-risk season. Campaign achieved 97% vaccination coverage in record time.
